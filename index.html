About Me

Seorang profesional berpengalaman di bidang teknologi informasi dengan spesialisasi sebagai Software Developer, DevSecOps Engineer, dan Software Architect. Selama lebih dari enam tahun, saya telah mendedikasikan diri dalam mengembangkan solusi perangkat lunak yang inovatif dan memastikan keamanan aplikasi dari tahap pengembangan hingga produksi. Keahlian saya mencakup berbagai aspek teknologi, mulai dari pengembangan aplikasi monolith ataupun microservices hingga integrasi sistem yang kompleks, sembari menerapkan prinsip-prinsip DevSecOps untuk meningkatkan efisiensi dan keamanan aplikasi.

Komitmen saya terhadap keunggulan telah memungkinkan saya untuk berkontribusi pada berbagai proyek yang berhasil meningkatkan operasi bisnis dan keamanan data. Saya percaya pada pentingnya belajar berkelanjutan dan selalu berusaha untuk memperbarui pengetahuan saya dengan teknologi terbaru. Dengan pendekatan yang berpusat pada solusi, saya berdedikasi untuk mengatasi tantangan teknis dan menyediakan nilai tambah bagi tim dan klien. Our Services

Custom Web Development

We specialize in creating custom web solutions tailored to your business needs. From corporate websites to complex web applications, we leverage the latest technologies to deliver exceptional user experiences and boost your business performance.

Application Security & DevSecOps Consulting

Integrating best security practices into your software development lifecycle, we offer DevSecOps consulting to safeguard your applications from development to production, reducing risks and accelerating deployment.

System Hardening

Implementing system hardening strategies, we enhance the security of your IT infrastructure. We assess and fortify system configurations, reducing attack surfaces and protecting your data and applications from security threats.

Software Architecture & System Design

We design scalable and high-performance software architectures. Our strategic system designs ensure your applications grow with your business, maintaining efficiency and manageability.

Performance Optimization & Scalability

Enhance your application's performance and scalability with our advanced technical solutions. We conduct in-depth analysis and implement optimal practices to ensure your application can handle increasing workloads seamlessly.

API Development and System Integration

Developing and integrating secure and efficient APIs, we facilitate seamless system communication. We ensure smooth data integration between your application and third-party services, enhancing functionality and flexibility.

Application Maintenance & Support

Providing comprehensive application maintenance services, we ensure your application operates optimally at all times. From routine updates and bug fixes to feature enhancements, we keep your application relevant and efficient.

Managed Hosting & VPS Services

Offering top-tier managed hosting and VPS services tailored to your business needs. We ensure your online presence is robust, reliable, and ready to scale. From server setup, security hardening, performance optimization, to continuous monitoring, let us handle the technicalities while you focus on growing your business.

Technology Training & Workshops

We offer training sessions and workshops on various technology topics for your team, from web development and application security to cloud technology usage. We help enhance your team's skills and knowledge, ensuring they stay abreast of the latest trends.
